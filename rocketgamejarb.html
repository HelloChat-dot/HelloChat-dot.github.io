Rocket Launch Game

Test your landing skills among the stars! Dodge mountains, battle gravity, and now face the ever‑present Jarbt.
(Choose "Enable Jarbt" for a spooky challenge!)


Controls: Left/Right arrows to rotate, Up arrow for thrust.
Land on the GREEN platform (solid & elevated) to win.
The BLUE platform is your safe starting pad.
Beware of mountains, the planet—and watch out for Jarbt!
If you stray too far, a red warning appears.
After a crash your ship explodes and drifts off; if Jarbt smashes you, debris flies everywhere!
Press R to try again, or M for menu.
